Technology

ZoneSure, developed by ViaSight, turns ordinary roadway footage into meaningful, real-time insights that help teams enhance safety, ensure compliance, and maintain full visibility across work zones.

1. Smart Mobile Data Collection

ViaSight’s mobile app lets field teams capture work-zone conditions using the device they already carry. It records synchronized video, GPS, motion data, and timestamps, giving crews full control to start, pause, or stop campaigns whenever needed.

2. Automated Visual Analysis

Once a campaign is uploaded, the system reviews every frame to identify key roadway conditions, work-zone activity, and notable moments along the route. This eliminates manual review and highlights what requires immediate attention.

  • Safety Indicators: Frames are scored to reflect overall visibility and general roadway condition.
  • Incident Highlights: The system flags unusual or risky situations with short video clips and GPS coordinates.
  • Work-Zone Detection: Cones, barrels, delineators, and traffic-control signage are automatically identified with condition and placement notes.

3. Interactive Route Visualization

All processed data appears on a clean, color-coded map that shows safety levels, incidents, and work-zone elements. Users can click any point to view its corresponding frame, video snippet, and recorded details.

4. Actionable Campaign Reports

At the end of processing, the platform generates a concise summary of the route—including highlighted events, observed work-zone devices, safety patterns, and areas that may require follow-up. These insights support faster decision-making and improved field operations.
